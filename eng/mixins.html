Production of mineral mixtures

Feed mineral mixture for is used for feeding cattle that help to maintain the health of cattle. With accurate composition, rich nutritional content, long shelf life and effective results.

Our company is a manufacturer of mineral mixtures for animal and occupies a significant segment of the market of Ukraine.

We manufacture products according to technology standarts "A mixture of microelements for livestock and poultry" , using technologies to obtain a homogeneous mixture.

Homogeneous mixture — is simply any mixture that is uniform in composition throughout.

Analyzes of raw materials and finished products are carried out according to standard procedures in our own laboratory.

Years of experience of our organization, availability of skilled professionals ensures the high quality of our products.

Quality of our products meets the science-based standards of trace elements in animal diets.

At all stages of the production is monitored for quantitative loading of raw materials in the production equipment and the quality of mixing.

The application of engineering techniques and the use of compatible materials to avoid the formation of lumps and caking products during the warranty period of storage.

The product range includes a blend of standard and individual recipes.

We can make science based calculation of the nacessary micronutrient blends.
