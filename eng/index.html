About us

Our activities

We are committed to providing all our customers with high quality products and excellent service!

The company “Dneprotehnologiyi”, registered in 2004 and in 2008 set up the production of fat-free sunflower lecithin. Since then, the company has made a great progress in production of dry sunflower lecithin with the use of modern technologies.
“Dneprotehnologiyi” produces dry sunflower lecithin in the territory of Ukraine in production quantities. The factory which produces dried sunflower lecithin is located in the East of Ukraine, in Dnepropetrovsk. The factory is equipped with advanced equipment that allows producing the products of high quality, meeting the modern requirements of not only Ukrainian standards, but also of the world, generally accepted standards, existing in the food industry.

The company “Dneprotehnologiyi” produces dry sunflower lecithin only from raw materials grown in ecologically clean regions of Ukraine. Our specialists carefully approach the choice of raw materials and aim at meeting our customers’ demands with high quality product.

Sunflower lecithin powder has been classified as a dietary supplement and dietary supplementation in people’s diet. Lecithin is widely used at the enterprises of the food and pharmaceutical industries; it is also used in the livestock sector of many countries.

No other related products like soya beans, peanuts, rapeseeds, etc, which eliminate the risk of allergic reactions among people who disposed to them, are processed on the company’s equipment.

Nowadays our company exports dry sunflower lecithin in the countries of CIS, Europe and Asia.
